life, still

info ↴

in 6 years i almost never returned to venice, and if i did it was only for a day or two. it was for my friends, for longing. after a devastating heartbreak i moved far away from "the most romantic city in the world", from the city who was actively making me ill. I couldn't stand anymore the claustrophobic calles, the same ones who offered refuge to two young lovers, and the wide lagoon, the one who offered us solace in times of turmoil. how long can sunlight stay warm inside a stone focuses on longing and acts as a reflection on how the landscape is influenced by one's emotions.
