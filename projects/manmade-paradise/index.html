Manmade Paradise is a photo exercise done at ISIA U.

The Adriatic coast of Pesaro was the setting of a reflection on the human intervention on the litoral landscape and the recent floods that hit central Italy. The project was presented as an editorial sextodecimo at the Rocca Roveresca in Senigallia for the exhibition "Camere Con Vista. San Benedetto / Pesaro. Andata e Ritorno.
